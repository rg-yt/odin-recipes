Key Lime Pie

Description

A simple key lime pie recipe that takes less than 10 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 reduced fat graham cracker crust
  • 1 oz package sugar-free lime gelatin
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
  • 1 (8 ounce) container fat-free whipped topping
  • 2 (6 ounce) key lime pie yogurt- fat-free

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Let cool slightly. If you donâ€™t like lime use any flavor of jello with whatever low-fat yogurt you like.
  2. Stir in yogurt with a wire whisk.
  3. Fold in whipped topping with a wooden spoon or spatula.
  4. Spread in graham cracker crust.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours
  6. Slice the pie into 8 slices.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 148 calories; protein 3g; carbohydrates 23g; fat 4g.
